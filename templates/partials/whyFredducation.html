Why is it called Fredducation?

I wanted to convey that the posts on this site are related to my learning experience, i.e., Freddy's education. When I considered my website's name, that didn't role off the tongue very well, so the play on the two words was born. Originally it was going to be Freducation with only one d, but someone already owned most of the top-level domains for that name. I've always been more of a Freddy than a Fred anyways, so Fredducation it was.
