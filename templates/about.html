About Freducation

The goal of Freducation is to encourage language learners to interact with their target language in the real world. I aim to accomplish this by sharing my experiences as I learn Spanish, specifically with the people, places, and media that I have learned so much from. I felt that the language learning community that I interacted with was overly concerned with things like counting the hours of listening comprehension exercises until a mile stone was reached, debating the usefulness of different learning apps, or how to attain a certain accent in a target language. I thought to myself “isn’t the point of it all to enjoy the language?”. So I made a space to show how I enjoy interacting with my target language, Spanish. Hopefully this pulls people away from what’s not important in their own language learning journey.

About Me

I’m Freddy Drew, creator of Freducation. Recently I decided to stop putting it off and learn Spanish. I thought it'd be nice to share how its going. I hope you like it.

Why is it called Freducation?

It’s not just a play on my name. It’s also a recognition that all of the posts on this site are about the many teachers in my language education. I do not consider myself an autodidact. I wish I could give credit to everyone and everythong that has helped me.
